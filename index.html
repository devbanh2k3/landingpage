After turning 40, I noticed that my once slim and petite figure had started to gain weight and bulge in unwanted areas.

I Was Ready To Get Back The 'Real Me'

The lady at my local health food store recommended me mixtures with pickle juice, lemon juice, apple cider vinegar, lemons, bananas, cranberries and more.
